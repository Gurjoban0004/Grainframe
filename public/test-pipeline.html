Visual checklist

  • Are the blacks lifted? (shadows should look slightly faded/matte, not pure black)
  • Are highlights compressed? (bright areas should be slightly pulled down, not blown out)
  • Are colors slightly muted and cool-shifted?
  • Is there subtle grain visible when you look closely?
  • Does it look like a film photo, not just a darkened or noisy version of the original?
